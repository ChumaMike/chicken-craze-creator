

# 1010 Grilled Chicken Dube — Restaurant Website

A bold, dark-themed restaurant website with black and orange branding for this Soweto-based flame-grilled chicken spot.

## Pages & Sections

### Homepage
- **Hero Section**: Full-width hero with smoky dark background, the 1010 logo (uploaded image), tagline "Flame-Grilled Perfection", and a CTA button "View Our Menu" in orange
- **About Snippet**: Brief intro — "Serving Soweto's finest flame-grilled chicken, ribs & dolo since day one" with location at 2231 Mahalefele Road, Dube, Soweto
- **Popular Picks**: Highlight 3-4 bestsellers (Full Chicken Meal, Half Chicken Meal, Full Dolo, 300g Ribs) with descriptions and prices in card format
- **Why Choose Us**: Grid of icons — Flame-Grilled, Fresh Daily, Affordable Prices, Dine-in & Takeaway
- **Order Now Banner**: Links to Uber Eats and Mr D for online ordering
- **Operating Hours**: Sun 8:00–18:45, Mon–Thu 9:30–18:30, Fri–Sat 8:00–18:45
- **Location section** with embedded Google Map and address
- **Footer** with contact info, social links, and copyright

### Menu Page (`/menu`)
Full menu organized by category with prices in Rands:

**Chicken**: Full Chicken R172, Half Chicken R102, 1/4 Chicken R53, Full Chicken Meal (2 Pap & Salad) R224, Half Chicken Meal R125, 1/4 Chicken Meal R79

**Ribs**: 600g Ribs R182, 300g Ribs R102, Chips & Russian R82

**Dolo (Eisbein)**: Full Dolo R152, Half Dolo R79

**Sides**: Pap R15, Chips R30, Salad R15, Atchaar R20, Chilli Sauce R15

Each item shows name, price, and a short description. Category tabs/filters for easy browsing.

- 4.3★ rated on delivery platforms

- **Colors**: Black (#0A0A0A) background, orange (#F97316) accents, white text, dark grey cards
- **Typography**: Bold headings, clean body text
- **Style**: Dark, premium restaurant feel with subtle smoke/fire textures via CSS gradients
- **Mobile-first responsive** design
- Sticky navigation bar with logo and menu links
- Smooth scroll animations
